Project Descripition

Auth Service :

The Auth Service is responsible for securing the system by managing user authentication and authorization. It handles user login and registration, generates and validates JWT tokens, and enforces role-based access control for different users such as Admin, Doctor, and Receptionist. This service ensures that only authorized users can access protected resources across all microservices

Doctor Service

The Doctor Service manages all doctor-related information, including profiles, department details, specialization, contact information, and availability schedules. It provides necessary doctor data to other services like Appointment and Receptionist services, enabling efficient appointment scheduling and hospital staff management.

Patient Service

The Patient Service stores and manages patient information such as personal details, medical history, and visit records. It serves as the central source for patient data, supporting appointment scheduling, treatment tracking, and operational workflows across the hospital system.

Appointment Service:

The Appointment Service handles appointment booking, updating, and cancellation processes. It coordinates between patient and doctor services to ensure proper scheduling while avoiding time conflicts. This service enables efficient management of patient visits and improves hospital workflow efficiency.

Receptionist Service:

The Receptionist Service supports front-desk operations by managing patient registration and appointment coordination. It acts as an operational interface that allows hospital staff to interact with patient, doctor, and appointment services, ensuring smooth daily hospital operations.

Salary Processing:

The Salary Processing Service automates monthly salary calculations for doctors and staff using Spring Batch and scheduled cron jobs. It calculates payroll components such as allowances, deductions, and net salary, reducing manual effort and ensuring accurate salary processing.
